About the Organiser:

The Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur is a public institution
established by the government of India in 1951. It is the first of the IITs to be established and is recognised as an Institute of National
Importance. In 2019 it was awarded the status of Institute of Eminence.

About the Event:

Khoj also called KHJ, is the annual techno-management festival of one
of India’s premier technical institutions, the IIT Kharagpur. Khoj’s journey started in 2004, from then its aim is to provide the technically
inclined youth’s generation a national-level platform, where they can show their talents and expertise and would be recognized and rewarded.

Since its early years, Khoj developed exponentially, it gets an
overpowering response from the students from various famous technical institutes and educational communities everywhere throughout the world.
Not only from India but students from foreign countries are getting attracted toward Khoj and are actively participating in its events creating a more
competitive, challenging and developing environment.

Recently Khoj’s 16th release had been held from 19th to 21st January
2019. In this edition, Khoj’s gets an all-over interest of more than 70,000 students from various technical institutes. And a total of Rs. 45
lacs offered as prize money. The Seventeenth release of Khoj is scheduled from 17th to 19th January 2020.
