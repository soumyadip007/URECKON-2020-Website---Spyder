We are wedding planners based in Howrah and together with you we can create authentic weddings in Howrah and Kolkata . We provide expert advise for any aspect of your wedding planning in Howrah & Kolkata : such as dealing with the never ending bureaucratic paperwork, sourcing local trustable suppliers (venues, photographers, flowers…) and also giving you creative ideas with an authentic twist!
Getting married in Kolkata is not complicated, but it could be a hassle if you do not know the local vendors or you are not familiar with the Indian culture. For these reasons we can help you in many ways. So do not hesitate to contact us and tell us your wedding idea – we will give you initial suggestions which will reflect your style as well as your budget.
